Tailored for you!

Curtains

The curtains cascade gracefully from a brushed nickel rod, their luxurious velvet fabric a rich, deep emerald green that adds a touch of elegance to the room. With a floor-length design that lightly gathers at the bottom, they create a flowing silhouette that softens the angular lines of the windows.

Pillows

The pillow is a plush, rectangular cloud of comfort, filled with soft, hypoallergenic polyester that cradles the head gently. Its cover is made from breathable cotton, quilted with delicate patterns that add a touch of elegance to any bedroom decor.

Curtain ties

The curtain ties are crafted from a luxurious ivory silk, exuding elegance and sophistication. Each tie measures about two feet in length, designed to gently gather the fabric of the drapes and create soft, cascading folds. A delicate tassel dangles from each end, adding a touch of whimsy and movement that catches the eye.
